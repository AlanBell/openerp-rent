OpenERP Renting Module
======================

OpenERP Rent is an OpenERP 6 module which let you rent your products to customer. Here is a list of features
available with OpenERP Rent :

    * Stockable products renting with stock management, including delivery order/incoming shipments creation
    * Services products renting, like appartement or web hosting management
    * Per-products rent prices and per product rent unities (Day, Month, year)
    * Different invoices periods, like Monthly or Once
    * Automatic invoice creation in background when needed
    * Easy to configure report based on Aeroo Report engine
